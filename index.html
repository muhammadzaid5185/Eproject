Origin & History of Calligraphy

Tracing the beautiful journey of written art through centuries

Arabic Calligraphy (c. 600 BCE)

Originated with the spread of islam, considered a sacred art form in islamic culture Widely used in the writing of the Quran and decoration of mosques .

Greek Calligraphy (c. 800 BCE)

Originated in Ancient Greece closely linked with the development of the Greek alphabet it played a vtal role in literature, philosophy, and art.

Indic Calligraphy (c. 1500 BCE)

Originated form ancient scripts of the indian subcontinent such as Brahmi it played a key role in religious texts, literature, and cultural expression .
